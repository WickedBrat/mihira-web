# Mihira Support

## Contact

For help with Mihira, email:

- `help@getmihira.com`

## Common Topics

### Account access

If you cannot sign in, include:

- the email address tied to your account
- your device type
- whether you are using iPhone, Android, or web

### Subscription issues

If you were charged or your subscription is not showing correctly, include:

- the email address tied to your account
- the platform used for purchase
- the purchase date
- a screenshot if available

### Birth details or profile issues

If your profile or birth details look wrong, include:

- the details that should be corrected
- a short description of what the app is currently showing

### Bug reports

When reporting a bug, include:

- device model
- OS version
- app version
- steps to reproduce the issue
- screenshots or screen recordings if available

## Response Time

Target support response time:

- within 2 business days

Set your actual support SLA before launch.
