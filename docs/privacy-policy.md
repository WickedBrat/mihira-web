# Mihira Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mihira collects, uses, stores, and shares information when users access the Mihira mobile app, website, support channels, and related services.

By using Mihira, users agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If a user does not agree, they should not use the service.

## Overview

Mihira is a spiritual guidance and Vedic lifestyle app. The product uses account data, birth details, preferences, user questions, astrology calculations, scripture-grounded guidance, sacred timing tools, analytics, and subscription services to deliver the app experience.

Mihira does not sell personal information. Birth details, spiritual preferences, and user-submitted questions are treated as sensitive product data and are used to provide and improve the service.

## Information We Collect

### Information users provide directly

Mihira may collect information users choose to provide, including:

- name
- email address
- sign-in and account profile information
- birth date
- birth time
- birth place
- language, region, guide, focus area, and other spiritual setup choices
- prompts, questions, chat messages, reflections, and other content submitted in the app
- support requests, feedback, and related communications
- subscription or entitlement status

### Information collected automatically

Mihira may automatically collect:

- device type and operating system
- app version and build information
- approximate technical identifiers used for authentication, analytics, subscription management, and session security
- usage events, feature interactions, screen views, and diagnostic information
- crash, performance, and reliability data where enabled

### Information from service providers

Mihira may receive information from third-party services used to operate the product, including:

- Clerk for authentication and account management
- Supabase for database, storage, and account-linked product data
- RevenueCat, Apple App Store, and Google Play for subscriptions, purchases, entitlements, cancellations, and billing status
- PostHog or similar analytics tools for product analytics and feature usage
- Perplexity or other AI inference providers for guidance, chat, and interpretation features
- hosting, infrastructure, monitoring, email, and support providers

## How We Use Information

Mihira uses information to:

- create, authenticate, and manage user accounts
- provide daily alignment, sacred timing, scripture guidance, profile, onboarding, and subscription features
- personalize the app based on birth details, preferences, saved profile data, and prior interactions
- generate AI-assisted guidance, interpretations, and recommendations
- save preferences, history, profile settings, and account-linked product state
- process purchases, verify entitlements, and manage subscriptions
- respond to support requests and user communications
- measure product performance, diagnose issues, and improve reliability
- understand aggregate feature usage and improve the service
- protect Mihira, users, and the service from misuse, fraud, abuse, or security threats
- comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements

## Sensitive and Spiritual Information

Birth details, spiritual preferences, deity or guide choices, user questions, and reflections may be sensitive. Mihira uses this information to personalize the product experience and does not sell it.

Users should avoid submitting information they are not comfortable storing in a digital service, including highly sensitive personal, medical, legal, financial, or emergency information.

## AI and Guidance Features

Mihira uses AI-assisted systems to generate spiritual guidance, interpretations, timing suggestions, and chat responses. User prompts, relevant profile context, and app-generated context may be processed by Mihira systems and AI providers to produce responses and improve reliability and safety.

Mihira is designed for spiritual, reflective, informational, and lifestyle use. It is not a substitute for medical advice, legal advice, financial advice, mental health treatment, emergency services, or professional counseling.

Mihira may use analytics to understand how users move through the product, which features are used, where errors occur, and how the app can be improved. Analytics may include event names, screen names, device context, app version, and account or session identifiers.

Mihira does not directly collect or store full payment card numbers for app-store purchases.
