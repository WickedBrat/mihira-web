# Mihira Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This draft is for operational use and review. It should be reviewed by counsel before public launch.

## Overview

Mihira is a spiritual guidance and Vedic lifestyle app. This Privacy Policy explains what information Mihira collects, how it is used, and what choices users have.

By using Mihira, users agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information users provide directly

Mihira may collect:

- name
- email address
- account credentials or authentication data provided through sign-in providers
- birth date
- birth time
- birth place
- profile preferences such as language, region, and spiritual setup choices
- questions, prompts, and other content submitted in the app
- support requests and messages

### Information collected automatically

Mihira may automatically collect:

- device type
- operating system
- app version
- usage events and analytics data
- crash and performance information if crash reporting is enabled
- approximate technical identifiers used for authentication, analytics, and session security

### Information from third parties

Mihira uses third-party services that may provide information needed to operate the product, such as:

- authentication providers
- infrastructure and database providers
- analytics providers
- billing providers

## How We Use Information

Mihira uses information to:

- create and manage user accounts
- personalize daily alignment, sacred timing, and guidance features
- save user preferences and profile settings
- operate billing, subscriptions, and entitlements
- improve product performance and reliability
- analyze engagement and feature usage
- respond to support requests
- protect the app, users, and business from misuse or fraud
- comply with legal obligations

## Sensitive and Spiritual Information

Birth details, spiritual preferences, and user-submitted questions may be sensitive for some users. Mihira uses this information to personalize the product experience and does not sell it.

Users should avoid submitting information they are not comfortable storing in a digital service.

## AI and Guidance Features

Mihira uses AI-supported systems to generate personalized guidance and spiritual interpretations. User inputs may be processed to:

- generate daily alignment outputs
- answer guidance questions
- generate sacred timing recommendations
- improve system reliability and safety

Mihira is designed for spiritual and reflective use. It is not a substitute for medical, legal, financial, mental health, or emergency services.

## Sharing of Information

Mihira may share information with service providers that help operate the app, including providers for:

- authentication
- hosting and databases
- analytics
- billing and payments

Information may also be disclosed:

- when required by law
- to protect rights, safety, or security
- in connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets

Mihira does not sell personal information in the ordinary meaning of that term.

## Data Retention

Mihira keeps information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

- provide the service
- maintain account functionality
- comply with legal obligations
- resolve disputes
- enforce agreements

Retention periods may differ depending on the type of data and the legal or operational reason for storing it.

## User Choices and Rights

Depending on location, users may have rights to:

- access personal information
- correct personal information
- delete personal information
- object to certain processing
- withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis for processing

Users can request account deletion through the app where available or by contacting support.

## Account Deletion

If a user deletes their account, Mihira will make commercially reasonable efforts to delete or de-identify associated personal data, subject to:

- legal obligations
- security and fraud-prevention needs
- backup and disaster-recovery processes
- technical or system limitations

Some residual information may remain in logs, backups, or records for a limited period.

## International Data Transfers

Mihira may use service providers that process data in countries other than the user’s country of residence. By using the service, users understand that their information may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

## Security

Mihira uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No system is perfectly secure, and Mihira cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children

Mihira is not intended for children under 13, or a higher minimum age where required by local law. If Mihira becomes aware that personal information has been collected from a child in violation of applicable law, it will take reasonable steps to delete it.

## Changes to This Policy

Mihira may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
